About the Department

I am part of the Information Systems department at Bahir Dar University. Our department is dedicated to providing a solid foundation in information systems principles and preparing students for successful careers in the tech industry.

We focus on equipping students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills. Our curriculum includes a mix of courses in database management, systems analysis, web development, and cybersecurity, among others. The department also encourages participation in internships and industry projects to gain real-world experience.
